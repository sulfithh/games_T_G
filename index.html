Welcome to Mini Arcade!

Get ready to play and have some fun! Choose a game below and challenge yourself or a friend. Whether you're into classic strategy or testing your luck, there's something for everyone. Let's play!

Guess My Number

Can you guess the number I'm thinking of?

  • The number is between 1 and 20.
  • You have limited attempts .
  • After each guess, you'll get a hint: "Too high", "Too low", or "Correct!".
  • Your score is based on how quickly you guess it.
  • Try to beat your high score!

Tic Tac Toe

The classic game of strategy for two players.

  • Players take turns marking X and O on the 3x3 grid.
  • The first player to get 3 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
  • If all 9 squares are filled and no one wins, it's a draw!
  • Click on a square to make your move.
